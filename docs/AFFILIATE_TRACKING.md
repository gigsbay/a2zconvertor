## Policy Reminders

- Do not resell Wondershare products on marketplaces like eBay, Shopee or Amazon.
- Do not use Wondershare brand or product names as Google Ads messages.
- Do not claim official sponsorship unless separately agreed.

## Placement Rules

- Keep A2ZConvertor's free browser tool as the primary call to action.
- Place affiliate cards after the main tool, related tools, or educational content.
- Clearly label affiliate recommendations.
- Use `target="_blank"` and `rel="sponsored nofollow noopener noreferrer"` on affiliate links.
- Do not add unapproved affiliate networks, shortened links, or invented tracking URLs.
- Do not place affiliate recommendations on irrelevant pages.
