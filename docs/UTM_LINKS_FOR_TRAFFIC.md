## Naming Tips

- Keep `utm_source` as the platform.
- Keep `utm_medium` broad: social, shorts, community, directory, email.
- Keep `utm_campaign` stable for a sprint so analytics groups correctly.
- Avoid putting personal data or secret partner IDs in UTM parameters.
