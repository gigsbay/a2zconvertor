# A2ZConvertor Revenue Plan

Target: build toward a sustainable GBP 1,000 per month without weakening trust,
site speed or the usefulness of the free tools.

These figures are planning assumptions, not guarantees. Revenue per thousand
pageviews (RPM), conversion rates and sponsor demand vary significantly by
country, season and traffic quality.

| Milestone | Monthly visitors | Monthly pageviews | Ads assumption | Affiliate assumption | Sponsor assumption | Indicative total |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| Month 1 | 1,000 | 2,000 | GBP 0 while UX and consent are validated | GBP 0-25 | GBP 0 | GBP 0-25 |
| Month 3 | 5,000 | 10,000 | GBP 20-50 at roughly GBP 2-5 RPM | GBP 25-75 | GBP 0-100 | GBP 45-225 |
| Month 6 | 20,000 | 40,000 | GBP 120-240 at roughly GBP 3-6 RPM | GBP 150-300 | GBP 100-250 | GBP 370-790 |
| Month 12 | 50,000 | 100,000 | GBP 350-600 at roughly GBP 3.50-6 RPM | GBP 250-500 | GBP 200-400 | GBP 800-1,500 |

## Month 1: establish measurement and trust

- Launch Search Console, GA4 and Clarity with consent-aware configuration.
- Publish the eight initial resource pages and three commercial comparisons.
- Submit the sitemap and monitor indexing, Core Web Vitals and 404s.
- Configure a real newsletter provider and test every signup location.
- Create an affiliate disclosure before activating tracked links.
- Apply to the top two closely aligned partner programmes.

## Month 3: improve pages that earn impressions

- Refresh pages that rank on page two or three with clearer answers and internal
  links.
- Publish two genuinely useful resources each month from Search Console demand.
- Test one restrained placement per page type instead of stacking adverts.
- Build a welcome email containing the most useful tools and privacy guidance.
- Begin direct outreach to five relevant software companies each month.

## Month 6: develop repeatable channels

- Reach at least 40,000 useful monthly pageviews before increasing ad density.
- Compare earnings per click and conversion by resource, category and device.
- Build sponsor packages for one tool placement, one resource and one newsletter
  issue.
- Refresh the highest-traffic comparison pages quarterly.
- Remove placements that create poor engagement or reduce user trust.

## Month 12: target a balanced GBP 1,000 month

- Avoid relying on a single source for more than roughly half of revenue.
- Maintain 100,000 monthly pageviews with evergreen updates and technical SEO.
- Target two recurring sponsors rather than one-off low-fit placements.
- Negotiate higher affiliate rates only after demonstrating qualified traffic.
- Review privacy, consent, disclosures and performance before enabling any new
  ad network.

## Monthly operating dashboard

Track organic clicks, indexed pages, pageviews, newsletter subscribers, signup
rate, affiliate clicks, affiliate conversion, revenue by page, sponsor pipeline,
RPM, Core Web Vitals and the percentage of revenue from each channel.
